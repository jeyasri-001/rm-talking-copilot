# Compliance & suitability checklist for RMs

## Every client interaction
- Confirm the conversation is with the **KYC-registered individual** (or authorised signatory).
- Log purpose and summary in CRM within 24 hours.
- If a recommendation is made, document the **suitability rationale** (risk profile, horizon, liquidity need).

## Product-specific guardrails
- **AIFs / PMS**: Minimum investor threshold per SEBI; accredited-investor disclosure required.
- **International exposure**: Confirm LRS headroom and TCS impact before execution.
- **Crypto / unlisted equity**: Not on approved shelf. Do not solicit.
- **ULIPs / endowments**: Only discuss on explicit client request — do not cross-sell from investment platform.
- **Tax-free bonds**: Secondary market only; confirm yield-to-call, not yield-to-maturity.

## Flags requiring escalation
- Client concentration > 25% in any single scheme.
- Leverage against MF units > 40% LTV.
- KYC refresh overdue > 30 days — **block new transactions** and escalate to ops.
- Unusual withdrawal patterns (> 3x typical AUM movement in a month).

## Risk-profile mismatch
If a client requests a product outside their current risk profile:
1. Offer a profile reassessment (not just a one-time override).
2. If they decline reassessment, require a signed **Risk Disclaimer** and log in CRM.
3. RM must add a note explaining why the product still fits.

## Never
- Guarantee returns, even informally ("this will definitely do 10%").
- Recommend a product purely because of commission / trail.
- Share another client's portfolio as a comparison.
